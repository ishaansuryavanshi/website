// Process
HOW YOUR ITEM COMES TO LIFE

Every product is printed fresh, layer by layer, in your chosen color — no inventory, no waste.

01
🛒
PICK A DESIGN
Browse 1000+ products. Choose your design, color, and size tier. Every order is unique.
02
🎨
CHOOSE COLOR
Select Neon Green, Electric Blue, Flame Red, or Void Black filament for your print.
03
⚙️
SLICE & PREP
Your file is sliced into hundreds of ultra-thin layers by our slicer software for precision.
04
🔥
PRINT IT
Hot filament is deposited layer by layer at 0.2mm precision until your item is complete.
05
📦
SHIP IT
Cleaned, quality-checked, and shipped to your door. Fresh from the printer to your hands.
